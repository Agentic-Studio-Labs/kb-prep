- First level: separate by AUDIENCE (Teacher, Student, Family/Caregiver).
- Second level: within each audience folder, group by FINANCIAL TOPIC (e.g. Saving & Goals, Credit & Debt, Insurance & Risk, Careers & Education, Investing).
- Anchor charts are classroom visual aids — keep them with the topic they support, not in a separate group.
- Assessment materials (rubrics, answer keys, final exams, projects) go under Teacher, grouped by topic.
- Family/caregiver home letters are take-home resources for parents — group by topic within the Family folder.
- Aim for 8-15 folders total across both levels. No folder should have more than 15 files.
