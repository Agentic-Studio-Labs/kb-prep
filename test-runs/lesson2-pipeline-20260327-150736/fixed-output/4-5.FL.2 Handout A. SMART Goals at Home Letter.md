Dear Parents and Caregivers, This week, your child is learning about SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound) goals in their financial literacy lesson! A SMART goal is a goal that is Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound—a framework that helps students turn their dreams into actionable steps.

To reinforce this learning, your child is bringing home the 4-5FL.2 SMART Goal Progress Tracker (Handout C) to share their goal with you. Your support in this process will help them stay motivated and accountable.

How You Can Support at Home:

##### ✅Review their SMART Goal Progress Tracker – Ask your child to explain their goal, why it matters

to them, and how they plan to achieve it.

##### ✅Ask reflective questions – Help your child think through their plan by asking questions like:

•

What small steps will you take this week?

What challenges might you face, and how can we work through them?

How will you track your progress?

##### Set a family goal together – Consider creating a shared SMART goal, like saving for a special

activity or completing a project as a family.

##### Celebrate progress! – Recognize their hard work by celebrating milestones, such as completing a

step on their tracker.

By engaging in this activity together, you are helping your child develop lifelong goal-setting and financial planning skills. Thank you for your support!

Thank you for your support, Your School Team
